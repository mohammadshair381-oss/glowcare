About GlowCare

GlowCare is a frontend-only demo of a premium beauty ecommerce experience. No backend, no APIs, no database — just vanilla HTML/CSS/JS with localStorage.

What makes it premium

  • Consistent card framing for mixed image sizes
  • Clean component system (navbar/footer injection)
  • Realistic multi-page flows (cart → shipping → payment → success)
  • Mock auth + admin panel + orders
